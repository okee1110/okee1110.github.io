D2L Redesign

The D2L redesign is a concept I came up with while using the original d2l app. I found using the first app to be very difficult to navigate around, making the users experience difficult. I then thought of a way to create my own app that would be much easy for me and many users to navigate around.

Supparel

The Supparel app concept was thought of when I thought of an easy, covid friendly alternative to shopping. This app will allow users to go clothing shopping without the hassle of even entering the store.

The Oddset app concept was made when I thought of a covid safe alternative to gambling. How the app will work is that you will be able to gamble in games, such as slots, horse racing, with crypto currency. The app is still in the beginning stages, but I am looking forward to finishing and working on the app in the near future.
